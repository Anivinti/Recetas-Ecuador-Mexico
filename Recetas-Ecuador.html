Pristiños with Honey

Ingredients

  • 250 g of wheat flour
  • 15 g baking powder
  • 50 g of pumpkin
  • 250 ml of water
  • 50 g of butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 200 ml of oil
  • Paper towel
  • 1 cup of golden berries (uvillas)
  • ½ cup of water
  • 100 g of white sugar

Steps to Make it

To make the dough
  1. The dough is the most laborious part, so it is important to have time to prepare it. First it is necessary to cook the squash in a cup of water, until it softens. Once it has cooked, remove it, from the water and mash it.
  2. Place the flour with the baking powder in the form of a volcano . In the center, put eggs, butter and pumpkin puree.
  3. Mix the ingredients with your hands, adding the flour little by little towards the center of the volcano
  4. In case the dough continues to stick to the surface, little by little, more flour is added until a compact but smooth dough is obtained.
  5. When the dough has taken consistency it is wrapped in plastic wrap and left to rest for 20 minutes at room temperature
  6. Divide into balls of approximately 16g each, heat the oil
  7. Stretch the balls with a roll and with a knife cut a line in the center and several cuts around the edges so that it takes it's shape
  8. Fry until they take a golden tone on both sides
  9. Remove from pan and put them over paper towel
    10. To make the honey
  10. Blend the golden berries with half a cup of water and reserve the juice
  11. Place the sugar in a large-based pan and put the sugar all over the base.
  12. Cook it at medium temperature. When the sugar starts to melt move the pot, to rock it without using in a spoon.
  13. When everything has melted, add the golden berrie juice.
  14. The thermal shock will turn sugar into candy. Let it melt again with the uvilla juice, keeping the pot at medium temperature
  15. Leave the pot in the heat until the composition has reduced and becomes a little thick
