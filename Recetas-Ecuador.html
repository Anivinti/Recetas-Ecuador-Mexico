Pristiños with Honey

Ingredients

  • 250 g of wheat flour
  • 15 g baking powder
  • 50 g of pumpkin
  • 250 ml of water
  • 50 g of butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 200 ml of oil
  • Paper towel
  • 1 cup of golden berries (uvillas)
  • ½ cup of water
  • 100 g of white sugar

Steps to Make the dough

  1. The dough is the most laborious part, so it is important to have time to prepare it. First it is necessary to cook the squash in a cup of water, until it softens. Once it has cooked, remove it, from the water and mash it.
  2. Place the flour with the baking powder in the form of a volcano . In the center, put eggs, butter and pumpkin puree.
  3. Mix the ingredients with your hands, adding the flour little by little towards the center of the volcano
  4. In case the dough continues to stick to the surface, little by little, more flour is added until a compact but smooth dough is obtained.
  5. When the dough has taken consistency it is wrapped in plastic wrap and left to rest for 20 minutes at room temperature
  6. Divide into balls of approximately 16g each, heat the oil
  7. Stretch the balls with a roll and with a knife cut a line in the center and several cuts around the edges so that it takes it's shape
  8. Fry until they take a golden tone on both sides
  9. Remove from pan and put them over paper towel

To make the honey

  1. Blend the golden berries with half a cup of water and reserve the juice
  2. Place the sugar in a large-based pan and put the sugar all over the base.
  3. Cook it at medium temperature. When the sugar starts to melt move the pot, to rock it without using in a spoon.
  4. When everything has melted, add the golden berrie juice.
  5. The thermal shock will turn sugar into candy. Let it melt again with the uvilla juice, keeping the pot at medium temperature
  6. Leave the pot in the heat until the composition has reduced and becomes a little thick

Fanesca

Ingredients

  • 1 lb of peas
  • 1 lb of corn
  • 1 lb of red beans
  • 1 lb of white beans<
  • 200 g of rice
  • 1 lb of pumpkin
  • 1 lb of sambo
  • 100 g of peanuts
  • 3 liters of milk
  • 200 g of dried cod
  • 3 white cabbage leaves
  • 3 white cabbage leaves
  • 1lb chocho
  • 3 white onions
  • ½ red onion
  • 100 g of milk cream

Steps to Make It

  1. Cook separetely the sambo, pumpkin and the cabbage, also each bean and grain. Toast and peel the peanut
  2. Leave the codfish in water, change the water every 30 minutes. At least 3 times Then remove the scales and let it boil in the milk.
  3. Sauté butter, achiote oil and white onion in brunoise. When the onion is cooked well, add the rice. Mix. In the cooking of the fanesca cooked zambo is increased. Leave a few minutes and then add the previously cooked Zapallo.
  4. Add the zambo and the pumpkin, wait a few minutes to add the cookked cabbage
  5. Add all the grains, and 1lt of milk
  6. Blend the peanut with milk, add to the cooking pan
  7. Use the milk of the dried cod to add to the fanesca
  8. Stir so everything is combined
  9. Add Salt and let it broil
  10. Serve with decorations: egg, empanadas and fried plantain
